A quick guide

Help with Falaq

You do not need to know how the technical parts work. Falaq gives you a simple place to ask Claude for help and see when it needs your decision.

What is this?

Falaq is a cockpit for working with Claude. You can start conversations, keep an eye on work in progress, and answer questions without using a terminal.

The three screens

  • Chat with Claude is where you start a conversation and continue it.
  • Mission Control shows every session and brings decisions that need you to the top.
  • Phone view keeps the essentials easy to reach on a smaller screen.

What “Needs you” means

Claude has paused because it needs your choice or permission. Open the session, read the question, and choose an answer. Work will continue after you respond.

Not sure? Choose the safest option, or ask the person who runs your cockpit before approving anything you do not recognize.

Starting a new chat

Open Chat with Claude, choose New Chat, say what you need in everyday language, and send it. Safe mode lets Claude work only in its designated area, protecting the rest of your computer.

Attaching files and images

Paste an image straight into the chat, or use the 📎 button to choose a file. Add a short note saying what you want Claude to look at or change.

When to ask for help

Ask the person who runs your cockpit if a session repeatedly stops, the cockpit says it is offline, an approval mentions commands or access you do not understand, or you cannot find the work you started.
